## Beginner Dosage Guidelines

### Start Low and Go Slow

For first-time users, begin with **one gummy containing 10-15mg** of kratom extract. Wait at least 2-3 hours before considering additional doses, as gummies take longer to take effect compared to powder forms due to digestion time.

### Factors Affecting Your Ideal Dose

Several variables influence how kratom affects you:

-   **Body weight and metabolism**
-   **Previous opioid tolerance**
-   **Empty vs. full stomach**
-   **Individual body chemistry**
-   **Desired effects (energy vs. relaxation)**

## Dosage Recommendations by Experience Level

### Complete Beginners

-   **Dose**: 1 gummy (10-15mg)
-   **Frequency**: Once daily maximum
-   **Duration**: Wait 4-6 hours between doses

### Occasional Users

-   **Dose**: 1-2 gummies (15-30mg)
-   **Frequency**: 2-3 times per week maximum
-   **Duration**: 3-4 hours between doses

### Regular Users

-   **Dose**: 2-3 gummies (25-45mg)
-   **Frequency**: Daily use not recommended
-   **Duration**: Monitor tolerance carefully

## Safety Considerations and Best Practices

### Avoid Common Mistakes

Many beginners make these critical errors:

-   Taking additional gummies too quickly
-   Mixing with alcohol or other substances
-   Ignoring individual tolerance levels
-   Exceeding recommended daily limits

### When to Consult Professionals

Speak with healthcare providers before using kratom if you:

-   Take prescription medications
-   Have underlying health conditions
-   Experience anxiety or depression
-   Are pregnant or breastfeeding

## Maximizing Your Kratom Experience

### Timing Matters

Take kratom gummies on a slightly empty stomach for optimal absorption. Consuming them 2-3 hours after meals typically produces the best results. Quality vendors like [Cora Kratom](https://corakratom.com) often provide specific timing recommendations with their products.

### Staying Hydrated

Kratom can cause dehydration, so drink plenty of water throughout the day. This helps prevent headaches and maintains the compound's effectiveness.

## Recognizing Your Limits

### Signs You've Taken Too Much

Watch for these indicators of excessive dosing:

-   Nausea or vomiting
-   Dizziness or drowsiness
-   Irritability or anxiety
-   Difficulty concentrating

### Building Tolerance

Regular kratom use leads to tolerance, requiring higher doses for the same effects. Prevent this by:

-   Taking regular breaks (2-3 days off weekly)
-   Rotating different strains
-   Maintaining consistent low doses
